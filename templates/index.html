This model is based on the ResNet50 architecture, a deep convolutional neural network known for its depth and accuracy in image recognition tasks. ResNet50 uses residual connections to solve the vanishing gradient problem, allowing for the training of very deep networks. This makes it a popular choice in the industry for its balance between performance and computational efficiency. Despite being introduced in 2015, ResNet50 continues to be relevant due to its robustness and the extensive pre-training available, which can be fine-tuned for various applications.
